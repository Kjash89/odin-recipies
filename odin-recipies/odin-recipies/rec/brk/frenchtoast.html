French Toast

Description here

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Eggs

Directions

  1. Whisk milk, eggs, vanilla cinnamon together in a shallow bowl
  2. Lightly butter griddle and put on meadium-high heat
  3. Dunk bread in egg mixture, soaking both sides throughly.
  4. Transfer to heated skillet cooking until golden brown on both side.(3-4 minutes per side)
  5. Serve Hot
