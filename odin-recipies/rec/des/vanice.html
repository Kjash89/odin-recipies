Vanilla Ice cream

Description here

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Whipping cream
  • Milk
  • Vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Stir sugar, cream, and milk into a saucepan over low heat until sugar has dissolved.Heat just until mix is how and a small ring of foam appears around the edge.
  2. Transfer cream mixture to a pourable container such as a liarge measuring cup. Stir in vanilla extrat and chill mix thouroughly, at least 2 hours.(Overnight is best.)
  3. Pour cold ice cream mix into an icecream maker, turn on the machine, and curn according to manufacturer's directions, 20 to 25 minutes.
  4. When ice cream is softly frozen, serve immediately or place a piece of plastic wrap directly on the icecream and place in freezer to ripen, 2 to 3 hours.
